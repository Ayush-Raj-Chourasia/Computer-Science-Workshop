Historical Monuments of India

Taj Mahal

An ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, a symbol of love built by Shah Jahan.

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi, this is the tallest brick minaret in the world.

Red Fort

A historic fort in Delhi, it was the main residence of Mughal emperors.

Gateway of India

An iconic arch-monument in Mumbai, built during the 20th century.

Hawa Mahal

A beautiful palace in Jaipur, known as the "Palace of Winds."
