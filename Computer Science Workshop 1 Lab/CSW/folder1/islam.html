Mohammad Shoriful Islam

  • Mohammad Shoriful Islam (born 3 June 2001) is a Bangladeshi cricketer who made his international debut for the Bangladesh cricket team in March 2021.
  • He made his first-class debut for Rajshahi Division in the 2017–18 National Cricket League on 15 September 2017.
  • Shoriful has gained recognition for his bowling skills and has been a leading wicket-taker in domestic leagues.
  • He was part of Bangladesh's squad for the 2020 Under-19 Cricket World Cup and has represented Bangladesh A in various series.
